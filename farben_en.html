🌈 The Secret of Colors 🎨

Why is the sky blue and a rose red? It all starts in atoms and with tiny waves. Let's solve the riddle of colors together!

1. How light is born in the atom

Remember electrons? When you give atoms energy (e.g., through heat or electricity), the electrons get very fidgety. They make a quantum leap to a higher orbit. But they can't stay there!

📖 The Smart Word: Photons
When an electron falls back from its high orbit, it must get rid of the energy. It spits out a tiny energy package: a photon. That's the light we see!

Electron falls back = Light package (photon) is released!

2. Wavelengths: The "Size" of Color

Light moves in waves, just like water waves in the sea. The distance between two wave crests is the wavelength. And exactly this length determines which color we see!

3. The Prism: The Rainbow Trick

White light (from the sun) is actually a mixture of ALL colors! When white light flows through a glass triangle (prism), the colors are "refracted" differently and sort themselves side by side.

A prism separates white light into its colorful components.
