1. SI-Einheiten: Die Zeit-Maschine

In physics, we almost always calculate time in the base unit seconds (s). If you get a task with minutes or hours, you must first convert them!

💡 Conversion Tip:
1 Minute = 60 Sekunden
1 Stunde = 60 Minuten = 3.600 Sekunden (60 * 60)

⏱️ Convert Movie Time

You're watching an exciting science fiction movie that lasts exactly 2.5 hours. How many seconds did you spend in front of the TV?

2. Base Units: The Luggage Check (Mass)

Masses are preferably given in kilograms (kg). However, things are often labeled in grams (g). To know how heavy everything is combined, you must put everything in the same unit!

💡 Umrechnungs-Tipp:
1.000 Gramm (g) = 1 Kilogramm (kg)
Beispiel: 500 g = 0,5 kg

🧳 Pack the Suitcase

You're packing your suitcase for your vacation. You have clothes (12.5 kg), shoes (800 g), books (1,200 g), and a toiletry bag (0.5 kg).
How many kilograms (kg) does your suitcase weigh in total?

3. Präfixe: Das Laser-Messgerät

Kilo means thousand (1,000), milli means one-thousandth (0.001). When you add lengths, they must have the same prefix, otherwise there's chaos!

📏 The Bridge Repair

A bridge is actually 2.4 kilometers (km) long. A section of 350 meters (m) needs repair.
How many meters (m) of the bridge are still perfectly fine?

4. Formeln: Der Geschwindigkeits-Blitzer (v = s / t)

Now it gets serious! The formula for speed is v = s / t (speed = distance / time). Sometimes you have to rearrange the formula:
Calculate distance: s = v * t
Calculate time: t = s / v

🏎️ Speed Trap Task 1: How fast?

A cheetah sprints a distance of s = 120 meters in exactly t = 4 seconds.
Calculate its speed (v) in m/s!

v = m/s
